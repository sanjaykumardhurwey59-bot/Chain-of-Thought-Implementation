## What is Chain-of-Thought Prompting?

Chain-of-Thought (CoT) prompting is a technique where the AI model is encouraged to think step by step before generating the final answer.

Example Prompt:

```text
Let's think step by step.
```

This technique improves reasoning accuracy and helps solve complex problems more effectively.

## Interview Questions

### What is Zero-Shot CoT?
Zero-Shot CoT is a prompting technique where instructions like:
"Let's think step by step"
are given to the AI without any examples.

### When is CoT most effective?
CoT is most effective for:
- Mathematics
- Logical reasoning
- Coding problems
- Multi-step analytical tasks
