## Problem 1

### Question
A train travels 60 km in 1 hour. How much distance will it travel in 5 hours?

### Prompt Used
"Let's think step by step."

### AI Reasoning
1. Train travels 60 km in 1 hour.
2. Total time = 5 hours.
3. Distance = Speed × Time
4. 60 × 5 = 300

### Final Answer
300 km

## Problem 2

### Question
If a shopkeeper gives 10% discount on a ₹500 product, what is the final price?

### Prompt Used
"Let's think step by step."

### AI Reasoning
1. Original price = ₹500
2. Discount = 10%
3. Discount amount = 10% of 500
4. 500 × 10 / 100 = 50
5. Final price = 500 - 50

### Final Answer
₹450

## Problem 5

### Question
If 3 students can complete a project in 6 days, how many days will 1 student take?

### Prompt Used
"Let's think step by step."

### AI Reasoning
1. 3 students complete work in 6 days.
2. Total work = 3 × 6 = 18 student-days.
3. If only 1 student works, time required = 18 days.

### Final Answer
18 days
